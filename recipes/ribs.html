Honey Garlic Ribs

Description :

Honey Garlic BBQ Ribs are a mouthwatering and succulent dish that combines the rich, savory flavors of barbecue with the sweetness of honey and the aromatic punch of garlic. These ribs are typically prepared by marinating the meat in a flavorful mixture of honey, garlic, barbecue sauce, and other complementary seasonings.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds pork spareribs
  • ½ cup honey
  • ¼ cup soy sauce and ¼ cup distilled white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda and 1 teaspoon garlic salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Slice ribs into individual pieces. Combine honey, soy sauce, vinegar, brown sugar, and garlic in a large bowl; stir until honey and sugar are completely dissolved, then stir in baking soda. The mixture will begin to foam. Transfer ribs to the bowl and turn to coat.
  3. Cover a cookie sheet with foil; arrange ribs meat-side up on the prepared sheet. Pour excess sauce over ribs and sprinkle with garlic salt.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour, turning every 20 minutes.
